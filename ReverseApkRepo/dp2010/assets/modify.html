2. Subtiles on HDMI output(BETA)

Some Samsung devices can play subtiles on HDMI output
Enable "Menu"-> "Settings" -> "HDMI output(Samsung devices only)"

Enable "Menu"-> "Settings" -> "Support ruby tag(beta)"

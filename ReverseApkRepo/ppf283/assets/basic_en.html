PicsPlay User Manual

With PicsPlay, catch your impressive moment in your photo! You can learn PicsPlay’s various features with following manuals.

1. Main Screen


①5 Menu Buttons (Camera/ Preset/ Tool/ Color/ Add-ons) and Save Button.

②Reset on the top

If you click the menu buttons, sub menu of each buttons will pop up.

In the middle of editing, you can go back to original picture with tapping the reset button on the top.

When you are done with editing, with ③Save on the right end of the bottom, the edited picture will be saved.

 

2. Camera

(1)Load a photo : Photos in the gallery of your device can be loaded to the editor.

(2)Take a photo : The embedded camera will be called. You can take a photo and it will be automatically loaded to PicsPlay.

※ Once a photo is loaded to the editor either from the gallery or from camera, the resolution of the photo can be chosen according to a purpose of use.

Fast(640x480) : optimized to the transfer among mobile devices

Normal(1024x768) : optimized to the use for e-mail, blog, and websites

Maximum(2048x1536) : The highest resolution It will take longer time in processing and larger space in memory

(3)User manual : It will show this User manual page.

(4)Setting : Set photo save location, visit the developer’s homepage(Jellybus), Facebook page of PicsPlay and more.

Widget settings

You can allow the widget to be added to the home screen of the device, and allow the status of Privacy Protection to be displayed in the widget (signaled by the background color of the widget).

Show widget

Allow / block the display of the widget on the Home screen of the device. If the check box is selected, the widget is displayed in the list of available widgets. If the check box is unselected, on restarting the device, the widget is not displayed in the list of available widgets and cannot be added to the Home screen of the device.

Show Privacy Protection status

Show / hide the status of Privacy Protection in the Home screen widget.

If the check box is selected, the background color indicates the status. A green background indicates that confidential information is hidden; a gray background indicates that confidential information is displayed.

If the check box is unselected, the widget does not show the status of Privacy Protection. The background color of the widget does not change in accordance with the status of Privacy Protection, and remains gray.

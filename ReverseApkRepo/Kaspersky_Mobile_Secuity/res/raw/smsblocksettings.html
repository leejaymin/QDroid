Device Lock

Device Lock lets you use the SMS command lock:<code> to lock your device remotely and prevent unauthorized access to your data (<code> is the secret code to Kaspersky Mobile Security on your device).

Outgoing SMS commands are billed according to the price plan of the other mobile device.

You can enable or disable remote locking of the device.

Enable Device Lock

Enable / disable remote locking of the device by SMS command.

If the function is allowed on the device, on receipt of the corresponding SMS command, the application locks the device and replies with an SMS notification.

Outgoing SMS messages are billed according to the price plan of your mobile service provider.

If the function is blocked on the device, the application cannot execute the SMS command to enable Device Lock.

Text when locked

The text of the message that appears on the screen of the locked device. A standard message is displayed by default.

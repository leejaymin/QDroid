Objective

The game starts with a box, about half-full with marbles. Your objective is to remove sequences of marbles of the same color. Single marbles cannot be removed. A sequence consists of several marbles of the same color touching each other.

Every time you remove a sequence, a few more marbles are dropped into the box - the amount depending on the difficulty level. Additionally, every few seconds, a single marble will be dropped into the box. The interval between these is dependent on the difficulty level.

Scoring

You get points for every sequence you remove. Longer sequences give exponentially more points, so plan ahead!

Some surprise marbles (see below) may also affect your score.

Controls

Remove sequences by tapping on them.

Tilt the phone left or right to slightly affect gravity.

Shake the phone to shake the box - this may help some marbles fall into place. This will cost you an extra marble!

Surprises

Every time a marble is dropped, there is a chance for a surprise marble. You will recognize it when you see one! Surprise marbles have various effects - some are bonuses, some are penalties. You can get anything between extra 5 marbles dropped and an extra life!
